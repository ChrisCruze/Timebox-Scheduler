# Timebox Scheduler Brief

## Mission
Deliver an AI-first day planning assistant that blends structured task metadata, energy-aware scheduling, and proactive LLM agents to give people clarity, focus, and balanced progress.

## Audience
- Individual operators, founders, and leaders managing complex workloads.
- Teams experimenting with AI co-pilots for planning and execution.

## Value Proposition
- **Save 40+ hours/week** by automating logistics, rescheduling, and prioritization.
- **Improve productivity by 60%** through AI-supported planning loops.
- **Reduce cognitive load** via structured metadata and calm, transparent UX.

## Strategic Pillars
1. **Structured Inputs** – capture 15 essential task attributes to inform decisions.
2. **Energy Alignment** – schedule deep work during peaks and admin tasks in troughs.
3. **Adaptive Agents** – monitor calendars, detect conflicts, and propose updates in real time.
4. **Human Control** – the AI suggests; humans approve, edit, and steer intent.

## Experience Principles
- Calm surface with clear explanations of every decision.
- Built-in 10–15% buffer time and recovery rituals.
- Accessible web-first experience with future integrations (mobile, calendar, Slack).
